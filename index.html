About

Hi, my name is Rachel and I'm currently a fourth-year student at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). I'm incredibly enthusiastic about marketing, content creation, and sharing ideas through videos, articles, photos, graphics, and social media outlets. I am always looking for new ways to understand different perspectives and to evoke compassion through my work. Feel free to contact me if you'd like to connect or view more work samples-I'm always excited to get involved in something new.
